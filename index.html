ABOUT

The Ninth International Conference on Industrial and Information Systems (ICIIS-2014) will be held at ABV-Indian Institute of Information Technology & Management (IIITM), Gwalior, India jointly with San Diego State University, San Diego CA, USA during 15 – 17 December 2014. The mission of the conference is to bring together academics and industrial experts in the field of Electrical, Electronics, Instrumentation and Control engineering, Computer Science & Engineering, Industrial Engineering and Information Technology to a common forum, where a constructive dialog on theoretical concepts, practical ideas and results of the state of the art technology can be developed.

VISION

The vision of the conference is To promote innovative research for the development of tomorrows industry.

MISSION

The mission of the conference is To bring innovative academics and industrial experts to a common forum in order to facilitate constructive dialogues based on theoretical concepts and practical ideas in the field of Electrical, Electronics Engineering and Information Technology.
