The IEEE Authors' workshop will be conducted by Prof. Sharma Chakrabarthy from The Unversity of Texas, Arlington, USA and Prof. Bhim Singh from IIT Delhi, India on 14th December 2014.
The objective of this workshop is to familiarize the participant about:
• Selection of Journal to publish the paper
• Style of writing
• Citation and role of impact factor
• Search engine
• Plagiarism issues
• Use of algorithm/techniques/methods/tools from one discipline to another
• Product related issues.
•Students/researchers and faculty members from colleges, scientists and engineering from Government and private organizations are welcome to register for this workshop.
